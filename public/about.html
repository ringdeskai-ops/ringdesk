About AiRingDesk

We built AiRingDesk because we saw how many brilliant UK businesses were losing customers simply because they couldn't answer the phone.

Our story

The problem we set out to solve

A plumber on a job site. A dentist with a patient in the chair. An estate agent showing a property. The phone rings — and they can't answer. The caller hangs up. They call the next business on Google. That's a lost customer.

We saw this happen every day across thousands of UK businesses. The solution — a full-time receptionist — costs £25,000+ per year. That's simply not viable for most small businesses.

So we built AiRingDesk: an AI receptionist powered by the most advanced AI available (Claude by Anthropic), priced for real businesses, with UK phone numbers, UK support, and UK data storage.
