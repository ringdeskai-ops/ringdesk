AI Script Setup
How to write the perfect AI receptionist script
▼
▶️
Video Tutorial: Setting Up Your AI Script
Coming soon — subscribe to our YouTube channel for updates
Step 1 — Go to AI Settings
Click AI Settings in the sidebar. Here you can set your AI receptionist name and write your script.
Step 2 — Give your AI a name
Choose a professional name like Sophie, Aria or Emma. This is what callers will hear when they call.
Step 3 — Write your script
Include your business name, opening hours, top 5 FAQs, how to handle different callers, and what to do if someone needs urgent help. Keep it under 300 words for best results.
Step 4 — Set transfer numbers
Add phone numbers for Sales, Support, Billing and Manager. When a caller asks to speak to someone, the AI will transfer them automatically.
Common Questions
How long should my script be?
Between 100-300 words. Too short and the AI wont know enough. Too long and responses slow down.

Can I change the script anytime?
Yes — changes take effect immediately on the next call.

What language should I write it in?
English only currently.

Which calendar does it book into?
It books into your primary Google Calendar by default.

Yes — if the AI captures their email during the call, Google Calendar will send them an invite automatically.

Existing appointments are not affected. New bookings will be saved to your Appointments dashboard but not added to Google Calendar until you reconnect.

Webhook / CRM Integration
Connect AiRingDesk to Zoho, HubSpot, Salesforce, Zapier and more
▼
▶️
Video Tutorial: Connecting Your CRM
Coming soon
What is the webhook?
After every call, AiRingDesk automatically sends the call data (caller name, number, AI summary, transcript) to your CRM or automation tool. This creates leads, contacts or tasks automatically — no manual data entry needed.
Step 1 — Get your webhook URL
Choose your integration method:

Zoho CRM via Zoho Flow: Go to flow.zoho.com → Create Flow → Webhook trigger → Copy the URL

Zapier: Go to zapier.com → Create Zap → Trigger: Webhooks by Zapier → Catch Hook → Copy URL

HubSpot: Use Zapier or Make.com as a bridge → HubSpot action

Make (Integromat): Create scenario → Webhooks module → Custom webhook → Copy URL
Step 2 — Add URL to AI Settings
Go to AI Settings → Webhook / CRM Integration → paste your webhook URL → click Save webhook.
Step 3 — Send a test
Click Send test in AI Settings. This sends a sample call payload to your webhook URL so you can map the fields in your CRM.
Step 4 — Map the fields in your CRM
AiRingDesk sends these fields after every call:

caller_name — Name of the caller
caller_number — Caller phone number
summary — AI summary of the call
status — completed / voicemail / transferred
duration — Call duration in seconds
transcript — Full conversation transcript
business_name — Your business name
Step 5 — Turn on your flow/zap
Make sure your Zoho Flow, Zap or Make scenario is turned ON. Now every completed call will automatically create a lead in your CRM.
Common Questions
Which CRMs are supported?
Any CRM that accepts webhooks — Zoho CRM, HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Monday, Notion, and more via Zapier or Make.

What is the secret key for?
Optional security feature. If set, we sign every webhook with HMAC-SHA256. Your CRM can verify the signature using the X-AiRingDesk-Signature header.

How quickly is data sent after a call?
Within seconds of the call ending. The webhook fires automatically as part of our call completion process.

What if my webhook URL changes?
Just update it in AI Settings and click Save. The new URL will be used from the next call onwards.
