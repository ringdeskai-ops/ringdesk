All new subscriptions include a 14-day free trial. No payment is taken during this period. By proceeding with payment, the customer acknowledges the service has begun and waives their 14-day cooling-off right under Regulation 36(1) of the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.
When to Issue Refunds ✅
• Service was not provided as described for a significant portion of the billing period
• Material technical failure on our part substantially prevented use of the service
• Customer was charged incorrectly due to a billing error
Action: Issue pro-rata credit or refund within 5–10 working days. Contact: hello@airingdesk.com
When NOT to Issue Refunds ❌
• Change of mind after 14-day free trial has been used

📋 Admin Manual Setup (for clients who call in)
Step 1 — Create account
Go to Admin → Customers → + Add Customer
Enter: Business name, email address, temporary password
Client will receive a welcome email automatically
Step 2 — Assign phone number
Go to Admin → Customers → [Client] → Manage
If client hasn't chosen a number: Use Twilio console to purchase a UK number
Then update the client's phone_number in the database via Admin panel
The number webhook is automatically set to: https://airingdesk.com/voice/incoming
Step 3 — Set AI prompt
Go to Admin → Customers → [Client] → Manage → AI Settings
Or ask the client to log in and go to AI Settings in their dashboard
Use the prompt template below as a starting point
Step 4 — Test the number
Call the client's number to verify AI answers correctly
Check Call Logs to confirm the call was recorded
Verify the client receives an email notification after the call

Our office hours are [hours].
Security / CCTV

For enquiries, take the caller's name, contact number, address and what type of security system they are interested in. For emergency alarm activations, transfer immediately to [number].
🔧 Troubleshooting
AI not answering calls? Check Twilio webhook URL is set to https://airingdesk.com/voice/incoming
AI giving wrong information? Update the AI prompt in Dashboard → AI Settings with more specific business details
Not receiving email notifications? Check spam folder. Enable notifications in Dashboard → Notifications
Call transfers not working? Add transfer numbers in Dashboard → AI Settings → Departments section
AI not understanding caller? Check call logs for transcript. If speech recognition failing, caller may need to speak more clearly or use a better phone signal
