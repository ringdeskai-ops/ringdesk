Pricing Manager

Edit plan cards and sync changes to all 2,565 pages instantly.

Quick Overview
The Pricing Manager is the single source of truth for all plan data across AiRingDesk. When you edit a plan and click Sync to all pages, your changes update all 2,565 static location pages, purge the Cloudflare cache, and go live within seconds. The homepage pricing section is the master template — location pages mirror it exactly.
Plan Fields Explained
Plan Name
Shown on all pricing cards, billing pages, and invoices. Keep it short — Essential, Starter, Professional, Business.
Monthly Price (£)
The price shown on pricing cards and charged monthly via GoCardless Direct Debit. Excludes VAT — VAT is added at checkout.
Annual Price (£/mo)
Shown when annual billing is selected. Formula: Monthly × 10 ÷ 12 = ~2 months free. Example: £29 × 10 ÷ 12 = £24.17 → round to £24.
Call Limit / Month
Maximum AI calls per client per month on this plan. When a client hits the limit, Aria politely directs callers to voicemail. Resets on billing date.
Features List
One feature per line. These appear as bullet points on pricing cards. Start higher plans with "Everything in [previous plan]" to show value stacking.
Most Popular badge
Only one plan should have this. It adds a cyan border and "MOST POPULAR" badge on the card — draws attention to your target plan. Currently: Professional.
Syncing & Access Control
Sync to all pages
Updates all 2,565 static location and industry pages with current plan data. Also purges the Cloudflare cache so visitors see the new prices immediately. Takes ~30 seconds. Always sync after editing plans.
Sync History
Every sync is logged with timestamp, who triggered it, pages updated, duration, and error count. Use this as an audit trail.
Admin Access toggle
Super admin only. Grants or revokes pricing edit access for each admin account. Admins without access can view pricing but cannot edit or sync. Toggle is instant.
Update existing clients checkbox
When ticked, changing a plan's call limit also updates the call_limit field for all current clients on that plan in the database. Leave unticked to only affect new signups. Reversible by changing the limit back.
Important: Homepage is NOT auto-synced
The homepage (index.html) is the master template and is never overwritten by the sync script. Edit the homepage pricing section manually, then run sync to push those values to all other pages.
