Frequently asked questions

How long does setup really take? +
Most businesses are live within 10 minutes. The longest part is usually writing the AI prompt — but with our website importer or quick-fill form, even that takes under 3 minutes.
What if I don't have a website? +
No problem! Just use our manual quick-fill form — answer 4 short questions about your business and Claude AI will write a professional receptionist script for you automatically.
Can I keep my existing phone number? +
Yes — you can forward your existing number to your AiRingDesk number for free. Your customers call the same number as always. We'll walk you through how to set this up.
Can I change Aria's script after setup? +
Absolutely — log into your dashboard anytime and update the AI settings. Changes go live instantly. You can update her greeting, business hours, FAQs and more.
What if I need help? +
Our UK-based support team is available Mon–Fri 9am–6pm. Email hello@airingdesk.com or call +44 20 4634 8499 and we'll get you set up.
