Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 March 2026 · AiRingDesk, a trading name of SatFocus Ltd. Registered in England & Wales · ICO Registration Number: ZB199222

🔒 AiRingDesk is fully compliant with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR). Your data is stored in the UK and never sold to third parties.

1. Who We Are

AiRingDesk, a trading name of SatFocus Ltd ("AiRingDesk", "we", "us", "our") is a company registered in England and Wales. We operate the AiRingDesk platform, which provides AI-powered telephone answering services to UK businesses.

Our registered address and Data Controller contact: privacy@airingdesk.com

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account information: business name, email address, password (encrypted), billing details
  • Call data: caller phone numbers, call recordings, AI-generated transcripts and summaries
  • Usage data: dashboard activity, feature usage, login timestamps
  • Technical data: IP addresses, browser type, device information, cookies
  • Visitor analytics: pages visited, visit timestamps, traffic sources, geographic location derived from IP address
  • Address data: business address and address type (business/residential) for phone number regulatory compliance
  • Payment data: processed securely by Stripe — we do not store card details
3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide and operate the AiRingDesk service
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send call notifications, transcripts and service emails
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to support requests
4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, our legal bases for processing are:

  • Contract performance — to deliver the services you've subscribed to
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our services and prevent fraud
  • Legal obligation — to comply with UK law
  • Consent — for marketing communications (you can withdraw at any time)
5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on servers located within the United Kingdom and European Economic Area. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.2+) for all data in transit and AES-256 encryption for data at rest.

We implement access controls, regular security audits, and employee data protection training to safeguard your information.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party processors:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI processing of call conversations
  • Twilio — telephone number provisioning and call handling
  • Stripe — payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Retell AI — voice AI infrastructure

All processors are bound by data processing agreements and comply with UK GDPR requirements.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectify inaccurate personal data
  • Erase your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, email: privacy@airingdesk.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the service, and optional analytics cookies to understand how the site is used. You can manage your cookie preferences using the consent banner on our website. For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we will erase your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law (e.g., financial records for 7 years under HMRC requirements).

Call recordings and transcripts are retained for 90 days by default, unless you configure a shorter retention period in your dashboard.

10. Visitor Analytics & Lead Tracking

AiRingDesk uses first-party analytics to track visitor behaviour on our website. This includes IP addresses, pages visited, device type, browser, and geographic location (derived from IP address). This data is used solely to understand how our website is used and to identify potential customers. We do not use third-party analytics platforms such as Google Analytics.

  • Visitor IP addresses are stored for up to 90 days then automatically deleted
  • We do not sell or share visitor analytics data with third parties
  • Visitor data is stored on UK-based servers
  • You may request deletion of your visitor data by emailing privacy@airingdesk.com with your IP address
11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by email of any significant changes. The current version will always be available at airingdesk.com/privacy.

12. Contact Us

For any privacy-related queries: privacy@airingdesk.com
For general support: support@airingdesk.com
