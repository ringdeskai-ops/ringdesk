FAQ

Common questions

Do customers know they're talking to an AI? ▾
That's your choice. Most businesses configure Aria to introduce herself as a "virtual receptionist" which is accurate and transparent. You can customise exactly what she says in her greeting.
What happens when a call goes over the plan limit? ▾
Additional calls will politely let callers know your lines are busy and offer voicemail. You'll be notified to upgrade. We never cut off mid-call.
Can I keep my existing phone number? ▾
Yes — you can forward your existing number to your RingDesk number, or port your number over to Twilio. Both options work. We'll guide you through it in the setup process.
How long does setup take? ▾
Most businesses are live within 30 minutes. Create an account, get a phone number, write your AI prompt, and point Twilio at your server. Our step-by-step guide walks you through everything.
What languages does Aria speak? ▾
Aria currently works best in English. Multi-language support (French, Spanish, German, and more) is on our roadmap for later this year.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Absolutely. No lock-in, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your billing dashboard and your account stays active until the end of your billing period.
