FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is it legal to clone someone's voice?

Yes — if you have their consent. Cloning without permission is prohibited by our Terms and, in many jurisdictions, by law. Every clone we create requires the speaker to recite a short consent phrase.

How long does the sample need to be?

30 seconds of clean audio is ideal. Shorter samples work, but the quality improves significantly up to about 3 minutes.

Which languages are supported?

English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Polish, Turkish, Russian, Dutch, Czech, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Hungarian, Korean, Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Malay, Vietnamese, Filipino, Indonesian, Thai, Ukrainian, Romanian, Greek, Swedish and Norwegian.

Can I use clones commercially?

Yes — on any paid plan, you own full commercial rights to generated audio (subject to the consent & ownership of the source voice).

How do you prevent misuse?

Consent verification, inaudible watermarking, usage logging, and a content-abuse review team. Reported misuse leads to account termination and takedowns.
