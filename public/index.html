FAQ

Common questions

Do customers know they're talking to an AI? +
That's your choice. Most businesses configure Aria to introduce herself as a "virtual receptionist." You can customise exactly what she says in her greeting.
What happens when a call goes over the plan limit? +
Additional calls will politely let callers know your lines are busy and offer voicemail. You'll be notified to upgrade. We never cut off mid-call.
Can I keep my existing phone number? +
Yes — you can forward your existing number to your AiRingDesk number, or port it over. Both options work and we'll guide you through it.
How long does setup take? +
Most businesses are live within 10 minutes. Sign up, pick your number, write your AI prompt, and you're ready to take calls.
What AI powers AiRingDesk? +
AiRingDesk is powered by Claude AI, made by Anthropic — the same technology trusted by enterprises worldwide. It's significantly more natural and context-aware than GPT-based competitors.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. No lock-in, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your billing dashboard and your account stays active until the end of your billing period.
