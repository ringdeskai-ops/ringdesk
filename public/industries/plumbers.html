Frequently asked questions

How much does an AI answering service for plumbers cost?
AiRingDesk starts from £29 per month with flat pricing and no per-call fees. The Professional plan at £149/month includes job booking and Google Calendar integration. No setup fees, and evenings/weekends are included at no extra cost.
Can an AI receptionist handle emergency plumbing calls?
Yes. AiRingDesk recognises emergency keywords like burst pipe, flooding, leak, no hot water, and gas smell. It escalates immediately via SMS or call transfer to the on-call plumber with the customer's address, contact details, and problem description.
What happens when I'm on a job and can't answer?
Aria answers every call instantly, takes the customer's details, books the job if it's routine, or sends you an urgent SMS if it's an emergency. Between jobs you check your phone — full transcripts and AI summaries by email.
Can an AI book plumbing jobs into my calendar?
Yes. AiRingDesk integrates with Google Calendar and books jobs through natural phone conversations — confirming the customer's details, type of work needed, and preferred date and time.
Will customers know they're speaking to an AI?
Most callers cannot tell the difference. Modern AI voice technology sounds natural and conversational. What customers notice is that the phone is answered instantly — which is exactly what someone with a burst pipe needs.
Can I keep my existing phone number?
Yes. Set up a call divert from your existing number to your AiRingDesk number. Customers keep calling the same number. Setup takes 5 minutes with your phone provider.
How quickly can I set up AiRingDesk?
Most plumbers are live within 10 minutes. Sign up, choose a phone number, add your services, pricing, and emergency protocols, and Aria is ready to answer calls immediately.
