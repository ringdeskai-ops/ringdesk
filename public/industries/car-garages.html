Common questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does an AI answering service for garages cost?
AI garage answering services like AiRingDesk start from £29 per month with flat pricing and no per-call fees. Specialist garage AI services with deep GMS integration range from £97 to £597 per month. Hiring a full-time receptionist costs £20,000–£25,000 per year.
Can an AI receptionist book MOT appointments?
Yes. AiRingDesk integrates with Google Calendar and can book MOT appointments, full services, interim services, diagnostics, and general repairs through natural phone conversations. The AI confirms vehicle details, checks availability, and adds the booking to your calendar automatically.
What happens when a breakdown call comes in after hours?
AiRingDesk recognises urgent language — breakdown, stranded, engine warning light, won't start — and escalates immediately via SMS or email alert to the on-call mechanic with the caller's details, location, and vehicle information.
Will customers know they're speaking to an AI?
Modern AI voice technology sounds natural and conversational. Most callers cannot tell the difference. What they notice is that the phone is answered instantly with no hold time — which matters more than who is answering.
Can I keep my existing garage phone number?
Yes. Simply set up a call divert from your existing number to your AiRingDesk number. Your customers keep calling the same number they've always used. Setup takes about 5 minutes with your phone provider.
Does it work with TechMan, Garage Hive, or other GMS?
AiRingDesk works alongside any garage management system. Calls are logged with full transcripts and AI summaries, and bookings go straight to Google Calendar. The Professional plan includes webhook support for connecting to external systems like TechMan, Garage Hive, or GDS.
How quickly can I set up AiRingDesk for my garage?
Most garages are live within 10 minutes. Sign up, choose a phone number, add your garage details (opening hours, MOT pricing, services, emergency protocols), and Aria is ready to answer calls immediately.
Does the AI upsell services during MOT bookings?
Yes. You can configure Aria to suggest additional services during booking calls — for example, offering a full service alongside an MOT, or recommending a brake check. This consistently turns single-service bookings into combined jobs worth more per visit.
