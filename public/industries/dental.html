Emergency handling

Dental emergencies, handled instantly

A patient with a knocked-out tooth or severe swelling can't wait for voicemail. Aria recognises emergency language — mentions of severe pain, bleeding, swelling, trauma, or dental injury — and escalates immediately.

Depending on your preferences, she can transfer the call directly to your on-call dentist's mobile, send an urgent SMS alert with the patient's details and symptoms, or provide immediate first-aid guidance while simultaneously alerting your team.

Unlike human answering services, Aria follows your emergency protocol every single time. She never forgets to ask a follow-up question, never misclassifies an emergency as routine, and never fails to send the alert.

Common questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does a dental answering service cost in the UK?
AI-powered dental answering services like AiRingDesk start from £29 per month with flat pricing and no per-call fees. Traditional human answering services from providers like Moneypenny or Best Reception typically cost £200–£800 per month depending on call volume, with additional per-minute charges on top.
Can an AI receptionist book dental appointments?
Yes. AiRingDesk integrates with Google Calendar and can book, reschedule, and cancel appointments 24/7 through natural voice conversations with patients. The AI handles the full booking conversation — confirming the patient's name, contact details, preferred time, and reason for the appointment — then adds it directly to your calendar.
What happens when a dental emergency call comes in after hours?
AiRingDesk recognises emergency language — severe pain, swelling, knocked-out tooth, bleeding — and either transfers the call directly to your on-call dentist or sends an urgent SMS and email alert with the patient's details and symptoms. You configure exactly how emergencies are handled for your practice.
Do patients know they're speaking to an AI?
That's your choice. Most practices configure Aria to introduce herself as a "virtual receptionist." The AI sounds natural and conversational using advanced voice synthesis — most callers cannot tell the difference. What patients notice most is that their call is answered instantly, with no hold time.
Is AiRingDesk GDPR compliant for dental practices?
Yes. AiRingDesk is fully GDPR compliant, ICO registered (ZB199222), and stores all data in UK and European data centres. Patient information is processed securely in accordance with UK data protection requirements. We never share patient data with third parties.
Can I keep my existing dental practice phone number?
Yes. Simply set up a call divert from your existing number to your AiRingDesk number — this takes about 5 minutes with your phone provider. Your patients keep calling the same number they've always used. No disruption, no confusion.
How quickly can I set up AiRingDesk for my dental practice?
Most dental practices are live within 10 minutes. Sign up, choose a phone number, add your practice details and FAQs (opening hours, treatments, NHS vs private info, emergency protocols), and Aria is ready to answer calls immediately.
Does AiRingDesk handle NHS vs private patient enquiries?
Yes. You can configure Aria with detailed information about which treatments you offer on the NHS vs privately, pricing for common procedures (check-ups, fillings, crowns, whitening), and any specific eligibility criteria. She'll answer patient questions accurately based on your instructions.
